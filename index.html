About

Building things.
Solving problems.

I am a self-taught junior developer based in Lagos, Nigeria, building towards full stack. I started with HTML and CSS, progressed to JavaScript and DOM manipulation, integrated my first live REST API, and have already delivered a production website for a real client business in Texas, USA. Every project is a deliberate step forward.

I bring something most junior developers don't: real professional experience under pressure. A year coordinating over 100 restorative justice cases at the Lagos State Ministry of Justice taught me documentation discipline, multi-stakeholder communication, and how to deliver when things get complex. Those instincts make me a better developer and a better teammate.

My background in International Law and Diplomacy means I write clear documentation, communicate precisely with non-technical stakeholders, and bring a structured, methodical approach to every problem I tackle.
