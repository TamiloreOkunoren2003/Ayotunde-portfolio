Legal & Operations

Coordinating 100+ Restorative Justice Processes Under Institutional Pressure

At the Lagos State Ministry of Justice, I became the operational link between facilitators, legal officers, victims, and community stakeholders — maintaining process integrity and confidentiality across a high volume of sensitive cases.

Outcome achievedZero procedural breaches. Improved scheduling efficiency and reduced last-minute cancellations through early briefing protocols I designed.

Designing a Digital Intake System for Restorative Justice Case Tracking

A conceptual project applying my web skills to the legal processes I coordinated. I mapped the paper-based intake process, identified pain points, and designed a web-based tracker using HTML/CSS/JS to replace manual case logs.

Projected impactEstimated 40% reduction in administrative overhead and a clearer audit trail for institutional reporting.
03
Development

Fetching Live Data: Building My First API-Integrated Application

The Weather App represented my first serious challenge with asynchronous JavaScript. I had to understand REST APIs, handle async fetch calls, manage error states gracefully, and present dynamic data cleanly — all without a framework or tutorial hand-holding.

Technical resultA fully functional weather app querying live OpenWeatherMap data, with proper try/catch error handling and a clean user-facing interface.

I developed simplified briefing scripts and plain-language summaries that explained complex international law procedural rights without sacrificing legal accuracy — a persistent challenge in cross-cultural restorative justice participation.

ImpactHigher participant confidence in pre-session evaluations. This skill directly transfers to writing accessible technical documentation and product copy.
